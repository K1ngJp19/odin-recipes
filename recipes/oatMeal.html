Blueberry and Banana Steel Cut Oats

Description

This hearty bowl of oatmeal is richly textured with a blend of steel-cut oats, blueberries, and dried cranberries, giving it a deep purple hue.

Topped with fresh banana slices and plump blueberries, it's a nutritious and colorful breakfast option.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 2 cups almond milk
  • 2 cups blueberries
  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 cup steel-cut oats
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Â¼ teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Stir water, almond milk, blueberries, bananas, oats, honey, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and salt together in the crock of a slow cooker.
  2. Cook on Low for 5 to 8 hours.
